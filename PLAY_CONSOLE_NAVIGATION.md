## 🆘 Still Can't Find It?

### Check Your Account Permissions
You need **admin access** to see API access settings.

**To verify:**
1. Go to Play Console
2. Click **Users and permissions** (search for it)
3. Find your account
4. Make sure you have **Admin** role

### Contact Your Account Owner
If you don't see API access anywhere:
- You might not have admin permissions
- Ask the account owner to:
  - Make you an admin, OR
  - Create the service account for you
